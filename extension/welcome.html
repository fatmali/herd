Welcome to Herd

Your tabs, organized. Your context, preserved.

⌨️ Keyboard shortcuts

Herd is built for speed. These shortcuts work from any tab:

  • Search all open tabs Ctrl+Shift+H
  • Open context recovery Ctrl+Shift+L
Tip: If a shortcut doesn't work, another extension may have claimed it. Remap at chrome://extensions/shortcuts (or edge://extensions/shortcuts).

📋 How it works

Herd automatically groups tabs by URL patterns — PRs together, docs together, design tools together. Click the toolbar icon and hit Organize Now to see it in action.

You can customize rules in Settings, or just let the defaults do their thing.

🧠 Context recovery

Came back from a break and forgot what you were doing? The recovery screen shows a timeline of your recent tabs with visual thumbnails — so you can recognize where you were instead of trying to remember.
